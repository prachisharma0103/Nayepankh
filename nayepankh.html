Our Story

We started in a pandemic.
We didn't stop after it.

"Service to mankind is the service to God. Let's revolutionise the society together."

— NayePankh Foundation
UP Govt. Reg. 80G Certified 12A Certified NITI Aayog Society Act 1860

When COVID-19 hit, a group of students from Kanpur refused to look away. While the world locked down, they stepped out — distributing food, essentials, and hope to those hit hardest.

That small act of courage became NayePankh Foundation. The name says it all: new wings for people who've never had the chance to fly.

Today, we run programs across food, education, hygiene, clothing, and healthcare — and we've touched over two lakh lives. But our goal is still far from done.

We're one of India's largest student-led organisations — because we believe young people have the most to give.
